How to Play

  • 1.

    Match Cubes:

    Tap to match cubes of the same color and complete the given tasks.

  • 2.

    Use Boosters:

    Combine cubes to activate bees, skunks, and other unique boosters for explosive effects.

  • 3.

    Rescue Animals:

    Free animals from cages, clear obstacles, and keep your zoo thriving.

  • 4.

    Collect Rewards:

    Earn stars to unlock treasure chests filled with valuable bonuses and daily gifts.

  • 5.

    Strategize Moves: 

    Plan your matches carefully to maximize points and progress through intricate levels.

  • 6.

    Replay Levels:

    Return to completed levels to earn missed stars or improve your score.

FAQ

  • How do I start playing?

    Download Zoo Boom, match cubes, and complete tasks to progress through levels.

  • How do boosters work?

    Combine multiple cubes of the same color to activate unique boosters like bees and skunks.

  • Can I replay levels?

    Yes, revisit completed levels to improve your score and collect missed stars.
